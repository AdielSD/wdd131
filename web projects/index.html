Lima, Peru

Peru is located on the western coast of South America and was once the center of the powerful Inca Empire. It is home to Machu Picchu, one of the New Seven Wonders of the World, and has a rich mix of ancient traditions and modern culture. The country is also known for its breathtaking landscapes, from the Andes mountains to the Amazon rainforest. Fun Facts: Peru is the birthplace of the potato, with over 3,000 varieties. The Nazca Lines, massive geoglyphs carved into the desert, can only be fully seen from the air and remain a mystery today.
